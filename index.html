How to Get Free Robux

1. Use "Pls Donate"

PLS DONATE is a popular Roblox game where players can donate Robux to each other. You can create clothing or game passes and list them for sale in the game. When someone donates to you, you’ll receive Robux after a short pending period.

2. Create and Sell Clothing

Design t-shirts, shirts, or pants and sell them on the Roblox catalog. If people like your design and buy it, you earn Robux. You need a Premium subscription to sell items.

3. Make a Game

If you create a game and it becomes popular, you can earn Robux through game passes, developer products, and ads. It takes effort, but it’s a great long-term method.

4. XBUX

XBUX is a free and fast way to earn robux! (and Minecoins)

XBUX cannot be installed on operating systems other than macOS.
